## Hard to Die 

@(dd Hard_to_die)
{ 
  "*Name": "Hard to Die",
  "*Brief": "Improves resistance from deadly wounds",
  "Category": "Advantage",
  "Cost": "1 TT/Hard"
}

When receiving a deadly wound, resistance checks are lowered
by one difficulty level (normal checks become easy, 
easy checks become trivial etc.).
